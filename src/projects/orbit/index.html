Research & concept development

The project started with lots of research and interviews with couples and a relationship therapist. Insights from this phase led to several unique concept directions, which were tested and iterated over time. This process helped shape Orbit into a focused and considered solution.

Design & experience

Orbit was designed to make planning moments together feel simple and enjoyable. The interface and interactions aim to support connection, without feeling forced or overly complex. The final result reflects the full journey from research to concept to finished product.
