The logo

The logomark is inspired by the director's “finger-frame,” capturing the essence of mise-en-scène and filmmaking. The central dot represents MISE's mission, emphasizing purpose and precision. Bold typography conveys confidence and professionalism, while the rounded corners add a subtle, youthful touch.
